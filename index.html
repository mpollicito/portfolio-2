About me

Hello! My name is Matt Pollicito and I am a very technology-based individual. I was born in Hartford, CT during November 1989. I have one sibling, an older brother, and ZERO first cousins (wild, I know).
I have always been passionate about creativity and expression of one's self. I've enjoyed drawing, painting and coloring since childhood, these interests are what got me where I am today!
During my high school years, I began taking graphic design classes to start my journey and have not looked back. I attended Tunxis Community College part-time after high school to lay groundwork for my future.
